The House

Welcome to Pine View Home — a modern Scandinavian-inspired mountain retreat on a private and peaceful 5-acre forested lot. With over 3,000 sq ft of bright, open living space and large south-facing windows, this home is designed for deep relaxation, gathering, and reconnecting with a slower pace of life. Whether you’re exploring nearby trails or enjoying quiet time in nature, this is the perfect base for families and groups to rest, reconnect, and enjoy the quiet of the woods.


Pine View Home is designed for comfort, connection, and family-friendly living. The main level features a gourmet kitchen with Bosch appliances and a massive quartz island, perfect for gathering before meals. The dining table with two leaf extensions comfortably seats 10. The living room centers around a modern Lopi wood stove with Greenstart igniter, creating a cozy atmosphere for chilly evenings. The main level also has an office with a queen bed and a full bathroom across the hall. Upstairs, the master bedroom boasts vaulted ceilings and an en suite bathroom. Across the hall are two additional guest rooms — one with a king bed and one with a queen bed — sharing a full bathroom. The garden level offers two more guest rooms: one with a king bed and en suite bathroom, and one with a queen bed and a bathroom across the second living room. Each room has a spacious closet and at least a 32" Roku TV, perfect for kids and adults alike. Guest Access & Amenities: - 3-car garage with ample storage for bikes, skis, or other gear - Fully finished laundry room with a second full-size fridge - 55" flat-screen TVs with Roku in each living room - High-speed Wi-Fi (300 Mbps)
